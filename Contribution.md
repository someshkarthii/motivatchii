### CONTRIBUTING.md

#### Overview

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **Motivatchi**, a productivity app that helps users stay organized and motivated by caring for a virtual pet. This document outlines how our team collaborated and provides guidelines for future contributions.

---

#### Team Collaboration

Our team followed a **collaborative workflow** using Git and GitHub to manage versions and integrate work efficiently.

* Each member was responsible for specific components, such as requirements documentation, architecture design, and quality analysis.
* Work was completed in **branches**, with **pull requests** reviewed by at least one teammate before merging.
* Weekly meetings ensured alignment on goals, consistency in writing style, and timely resolution of merge conflicts.
* Major design and requirement decisions were documented through group discussions and recorded in shared meeting notes.
